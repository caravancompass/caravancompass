Day by Day
Itinerary
✈
Day 1
Arrival in Tbilisi
  • Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Free time to relax or explore the city at your own pace
Overnight in Tbilisi
🏛
Day 2
Tbilisi City Tour
  • Old Tbilisi walking tour — Freedom Square, Metekhi Church, Abanotubani sulphur bath district, carved wooden balconies
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress with panoramic city views
  • Funicular to Mtatsminda Park
  • Visit the Chronicles of Georgia monument
Overnight in Tbilisi
⛰
Day 3
Ananuri — Zhinvali — Gudauri
  • Scenic drive along the Georgian Military Highway
  • Photo stop at Ananuri Fortress complex and the turquoise Zhinvali Reservoir
  • Continue to Gudauri — visit the Soviet-era Friendship Monument viewpoint at 2,395m
  • Check-in at Gudauri ski resort (leisure time at the resort)
Overnight in Gudauri
🏔
Day 4
Kazbegi Mountain Tour
  • Drive to Stepantsminda (Kazbegi) through dramatic mountain landscapes
  • 4×4 jeep ride to Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m (subject to weather conditions)
  • Views of Mt. Kazbek at 5,047m
  • Return to Gudauri for leisure time at the winter resort
Overnight in Gudauri
🏺
Day 5
Gori — Uplistsikhe — Borjomi — Bakuriani
  • Visit the Stalin Museum in Gori (exterior or interior as per group preference)
  • Explore Uplistsikhe — an ancient rock-cut cave town occupied from the Bronze Age to the 13th century
  • Continue via Borjomi Mineral Water Park (taste the famous mineral spring water)
  • Arrive at Bakuriani — alpine resort town
Overnight in Bakuriani
⛪
Day 6
Bakuriani — Mtskheta — Tbilisi
  • Free morning at leisure in Bakuriani
  • Drive to Mtskheta — Georgia's ancient capital and UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Visit Jvari Monastery (6th century) and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral (11th century)
  • Continue to Tbilisi — evening free for shopping or relaxation
Overnight in Tbilisi
🍷
Day 7
Kakheti — Wine Region
  • Drive to Sighnaghi — the walled "City of Love" with panoramic Caucasus valley views
  • Visit Bodbe Monastery, burial site of St. Nino
  • Traditional wine tasting experience at Vineria winery
  • Return to Tbilisi for the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
🛫
Day 8
Departure from Tbilisi
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Tbilisi International Airport
  • Departure

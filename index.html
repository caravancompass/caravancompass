Who We Are

Caravan Compass is a young, trade-focused Destination Management Company built around one simple idea: make it easy and safe for Indian travel professionals to sell CIS and nearby destinations.

We work only B2B, acting as your back-end partner — from itinerary design and costings to on-ground execution and 24x7 support. Our approach is transparent, detail-oriented and tailored to your brand’s way of working.

  • Curated experiences for CIS and select surrounding regions
  • Flexible support for FIT, groups, MICE and custom requests
  • Local ground partners vetted for reliability and response time

Why Work With Caravan Compass?

  • Fast, clear B2B quotations with clean inclusions/exclusions
  • On-trip assistance via WhatsApp for your travellers
  • Support for branding the experience under your agency name
  • Possibility to align with your preferred hotel/category style

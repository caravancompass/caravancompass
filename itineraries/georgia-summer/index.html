Day by Day
Itinerary
✈
Day 1
Arrival in Tbilisi
  • Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport — meet and greet on arrival, assistance through immigration and baggage collection
  • Private transfer to hotel — check-in (standard time 14:00–15:00)
  • Free time to freshen up and explore at your own pace
Overnight in Tbilisi
🏛
Day 2
Tbilisi Full Day
  • Old Tbilisi walking tour from Freedom Square — Clock Tower, Bridge of Peace, Rike Park, Metekhi Church
  • Cable car up to Narikala Fortress — panoramic city views, "Mother of Kartli" statue, Abanotubani sulphur bath district
  • Rustaveli Avenue stroll — shopping at Flea Market and Gallery Mall
  • Cable car to Mtatsminda Park (approx. 4-minute ride) — leisure time at the park, funicular ride back down to city centre
Overnight in Tbilisi
⛰
Day 3
Gudauri & Kazbegi Mountain Tour
  • Early departure (approx. 09:00) — drive through Mtskheta-Mtianeti Region along the Georgian Military Highway
  • Ananuri Fortress complex and Zhinvali Reservoir — photo stop
  • Gudauri viewpoint — Soviet-era Friendship Monument, mountain panorama
  • Drive to Stepantsminda (Kazbegi) — transfer to 4x4 vehicle for ascent to Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m
  • Return to Tbilisi in the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
🏞
Day 4
Tbilisi to Batumi via Martvili Canyon
  • Departure approx. 10:00 — drive west toward Batumi through Imereti and Samegrelo regions
  • Martvili Canyon — boat ride through the jade-green gorge, waterfalls cascading from the canyon walls
  • Continue to Batumi — check-in at hotel on the Black Sea coast
  • Free evening stroll along the Batumi Boulevard seafront
Overnight in Batumi
🌊
Day 5
Batumi City Tour & Makhuntseti Waterfall
  • Batumi city tour — Piazza Square, Alphabet Tower, Ali & Nino kinetic sculpture, Medea Square, Poseidon Statue
  • Cable car ride (approx. 2km) up the hill — panoramic views over the Black Sea and the Caucasus behind
  • Free time at the seaside beach
  • Drive to Makhuntseti Waterfall in the Adjara countryside — one of the most impressive waterfalls in western Georgia
Overnight in Batumi
🦷
Day 6
Return to Tbilisi via Prometheus Cave
  • Check-out from Batumi hotel (approx. 09:00)
  • Drive east toward Tbilisi through Imereti
  • Prometheus Cave, Tskaltubo — over 1km of illuminated chambers, stalactites, stalagmites, underground rivers and lakes
  • Arrive Tbilisi — hotel check-in and evening at leisure
Overnight in Tbilisi
🛫
Day 7
Departure from Tbilisi
  • Breakfast at hotel — check-out (standard time 12:00)
  • Private transfer to Tbilisi International Airport (3–3.5 hours prior to departure flight)

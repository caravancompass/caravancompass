Day by Day
Itinerary
Day 1
Arrival in Tbilisi
  • Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private minivan transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Free time to rest and explore the city at your own pace
  • Optional: evening stroll through the Old Town or along Rustaveli Avenue
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 2
Tbilisi City Tour & Mtskheta
  • Mtskheta — Georgia's ancient capital, 20km from Tbilisi: Jvari Monastery (6th century, UNESCO) perched above the confluence of the Aragvi and Kura rivers; Svetitskhoveli Cathedral (11th century, UNESCO), the spiritual centre of the Georgian Orthodox Church
  • Return to Tbilisi — Old Town walking tour: Freedom Square, Clock Tower, Bridge of Peace, Rike Park, Metekhi Church
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress — panoramic views over the Old Town and Kura River
  • Abanotubani — the sulphur bath district with carved wooden balconies
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 3
Ananuri – Gudauri – Kazbegi
  • Early departure (approx. 09:00) — scenic drive along the Georgian Military Highway through the Greater Caucasus
  • Ananuri Fortress complex and Zhinvali Reservoir — one of the most photographed spots in Georgia
  • Gudauri — Friendship Monument viewpoint at 2,395m; sweeping mountain panorama
  • Stepantsminda (Kazbegi) — transfer to 4x4 vehicle for ascent to Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m, with Mt. Kazbek (5,047m) as backdrop
  • Return to Tbilisi in the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 4
Kakheti Wine Region — KTW Winery & Sighnaghi
  • Drive to Kakheti — Georgia's primary wine region, home to over 500 indigenous grape varieties and 8,000 years of winemaking history
  • Wine tasting at KTW Winery — qvevri cellar tour and traditional amber wine tasting
  • Sighnaghi — the walled "City of Love" with panoramic Alazani Valley views
  • Bodbe Monastery — burial site of St. Nino, set in forested grounds above the valley
  • Return to Tbilisi in the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 5
Departure from Tbilisi
  • Breakfast at hotel — check-out
  • Private minivan transfer to Tbilisi International Airport

Day by Day
Itinerary
Day 1
Georgia
Arrival in Tbilisi
  • Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private minivan transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Free time to rest and explore the city at your own pace
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 2
Georgia
Tbilisi City Tour
  • Old Tbilisi walking tour — Freedom Square, Clock Tower, Bridge of Peace, Rike Park, Metekhi Church
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress — panoramic views, "Mother of Kartli" statue
  • Abanotubani sulphur bath district
  • Funicular to Mtatsminda Park — free time and funicular ride back down
  • Rustaveli Avenue stroll
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 3
Georgia
Ananuri – Gudauri – Kazbegi
  • Scenic drive along the Georgian Military Highway
  • Ananuri Fortress complex and Zhinvali Reservoir photo stop
  • Gudauri — Friendship Monument viewpoint at 2,395m
  • Drive to Stepantsminda (Kazbegi) — 4x4 jeep ascent to Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m
  • Views of Mt. Kazbek at 5,047m — return to Tbilisi in the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 4
Georgia
Kakheti Wine Region — Sighnaghi & Winery
  • Drive to Sighnaghi — the walled "City of Love" with panoramic Caucasus valley views
  • Bodbe Monastery — burial site of St. Nino, one of Georgia's most sacred pilgrimage sites
  • Wine tasting at Vineria Winery or KTW Winery — Kakheti amber wines in a working cellar
  • Return to Tbilisi in the evening
Overnight in Tbilisi
Day 5
Transit Day
Tbilisi to Baku
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Tbilisi International Airport
  • Flight to Baku (not included — arrange via your preferred carrier; Tbilisi–Baku operates multiple daily flights)
  • Arrive at Heydar Aliyev International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to Baku hotel and check-in
  • Evening at leisure — Caspian Corniche walk or Flame Towers viewing at dusk
Overnight in Baku
Day 6
Azerbaijan
Baku City Tour
  • Icheri Sheher (Walled Old City) walking tour — Maiden Tower, Palace of the Shirvanshahs, medieval caravanserai lanes (UNESCO World Heritage)
  • Fountain Square and Nizami Street
  • Flame Towers and Caspian Corniche seafront
  • Bibi-Heybat Mosque on the Caspian shore
Overnight in Baku
Day 7
Azerbaijan
Absheron Peninsula & Gobustan
  • Ateshgah of Baku — Hindu-Zoroastrian fire temple with Sanskrit inscriptions, built by Indian Silk Road merchants
  • Yanar Dag — the eternally burning hillside fuelled by natural gas
  • Gobustan National Park — 6,000+ rock carvings dating back 40,000 years (UNESCO World Heritage) — Gobustan Museum entry
  • Gobustan mud volcano field
Overnight in Baku
Day 8
Azerbaijan
Gabala Day Trip
  • Scenic drive to Gabala mountain resort town (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Nohur Lake — turquoise glacial lake in pine forest
  • Tufandag 2-line cable car — panoramic views of the Greater Caucasus range
  • Gabala town leisure — local lunch, resort stroll
  • Return to Baku in the evening
Overnight in Baku
Day 9
Azerbaijan
Departure from Baku
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Heydar Aliyev International Airport

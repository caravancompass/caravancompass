Day by Day
Itinerary
Day 1
Arrival in Baku
  • Arrive at Heydar Aliyev International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Evening stroll along the Caspian Corniche or Flame Towers viewing at dusk
Overnight in Baku
Day 2
Baku City Tour
  • Icheri Sheher (Walled Old City) walking tour — Maiden Tower, Palace of the Shirvanshahs, medieval caravanserai
  • Explore the narrow lanes of the Old City — carpet shops, tea houses, ancient mosques
  • Fountain Square, Nizami Street shopping boulevard
  • Flame Towers evening illumination from the Corniche waterfront
Overnight in Baku
Day 3
Absheron Peninsula & Gobustan
  • Ateshgah of Baku — 17th-century Hindu-Zoroastrian fire temple with Sanskrit inscriptions, built by Indian Silk Road merchants
  • Yanar Dag — a hillside that has burned continuously for decades, fuelled by natural gas seeping from the earth
  • Gobustan National Park — over 6,000 rock carvings dating back 40,000 years (UNESCO World Heritage)
  • Gobustan mud volcano field — Azerbaijan has more of the world's mud volcanoes than any other country
Overnight in Baku
Day 4
Gabala Day Trip
  • Scenic drive to Gabala — mountain resort town in the foothills of the Greater Caucasus (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Nohur Lake — turquoise glacial lake surrounded by pine forest
  • Tufandag cable car — panoramic views over the Caucasus range
  • Gabala town — stroll through the resort centre, local lunch
  • Return to Baku in the evening
Overnight in Baku
Day 5
Departure from Baku
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Heydar Aliyev International Airport

Day by Day
Itinerary
✈
Day 1
Arrival in Baku
  • Arrive at Heydar Aliyev International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Evening at leisure — Caspian Corniche walk or Flame Towers viewing
Overnight in Baku
🏛
Day 2
Baku City Tour
  • Icheri Sheher (Walled Old City) — Maiden Tower, Palace of the Shirvanshahs, medieval caravanserai lanes
  • Fountain Square and Nizami Street
  • Bibi-Heybat Mosque on the Caspian shore (original 13th century, rebuilt 1998)
  • Flame Towers and Corniche waterfront evening
Overnight in Baku
🌋
Day 3
Absheron Peninsula & Gobustan
  • Ateshgah of Baku — ancient Hindu-Zoroastrian fire temple with Sanskrit inscriptions, built by Indian Silk Road merchants
  • Yanar Dag — the eternally burning hillside
  • Gobustan National Park — 6,000+ rock carvings (UNESCO World Heritage)
  • Gobustan mud volcano field — slowly bubbling lunar landscape
Overnight in Baku
⛰
Day 4
Gabala Day Trip
  • Scenic drive to Gabala through the Caucasus foothills (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Nohur Lake — turquoise glacial lake in pine forest
  • Tufandag cable car — mountain panorama views
  • Gabala town leisure and local lunch
Overnight in Gabala
🏯
Day 5
Sheki — Silk Road City
  • Drive from Gabala to Sheki (approx. 1 hour)
  • Khan's Summer Palace (1762) — built entirely without nails; shebeke stained glass windows with 5,200 hand-fitted pieces each
  • Sheki Silk Road caravanserai — a functioning rest stop for merchants between Europe and Central Asia
  • Sheki Old Town — local crafts, walnut halva, traditional tea houses
Overnight in Gabala
🎨
Day 6
Return to Baku — Candy Cane Mountains
  • Drive back toward Baku via the Lahij road
  • Stop at the Candy Cane mountains (Khizi District) — striped red and white hillside formations unique to Azerbaijan
  • Optional stop: Lahij — a medieval copper-craft village
  • Arrive Baku — evening free for shopping or dinner on the Corniche
Overnight in Baku
🛫
Day 7
Departure from Baku
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Heydar Aliyev International Airport
  • Departure

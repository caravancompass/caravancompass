Day by Day
Itinerary
✈
Day 1
Arrival & Leisure
  • Arrive at Almaty International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to city-centre hotel and check-in
  • Full day at leisure — rest and unwind after the flight
  • Optional: evening walk along Arbat Street or Panfilov Park
Overnight in Almaty
🏔
Day 2
High Mountains — Medeu & Shymbulak
  • Full day dedicated to the mountains above Almaty
  • Medeu Gorge — visit the iconic Medeu ice rink at 1,691m, the world's highest speed skating rink
  • 3-tiered cable car ascent to Shymbulak Mountain Resort
  • Reach Level 3 at 3,200m — snow activities (seasonal), Tian Shan panorama, mountain restaurant lunch
  • Return to Almaty in the afternoon
Overnight in Almaty
🏜
Day 3
Almaty to Kolsai via Charyn Canyon
  • Morning check-out from Almaty hotel — drive 3 hours east into the steppe
  • Charyn Canyon — hike the "Valley of Castles" section where sandstone towers and spires rise up to 150m; one of Central Asia's most dramatic landscapes
  • Photo stop at Black Canyon overlook
  • Continue drive to Saty Village in the Kolsai mountain region
  • Check-in at boutique eco-lodge or premium guesthouse
  • Traditional Kazakh dinner at the lodge
Overnight in Saty / Kolsai region
💧
Day 4
The Lake Jewels — Kaindy & Kolsai
  • Morning 4x4 excursion to Kaindy Lake — a submerged forest created by a 1911 earthquake, where the bare trunks of Schrenk spruce trees rise from the crystal-clear glacial water
  • Afternoon visit to Lower Kolsai Lake — the most accessible of the three Kolsai Lakes, surrounded by spruce forest and meadows
  • Boat or catamaran ride on Lower Kolsai Lake
  • Return to the lodge for dinner
Overnight in Saty / Kolsai region
🦅
Day 5
Falconry Show & Return to Almaty
  • Morning check-out from the lodge — begin drive back toward Almaty
  • En-route stop for a traditional Nomad Falconry and Hunting Show at a local hawk farm — golden eagles, falcons, and Harris hawks demonstrated by Kazakh hunters in traditional dress
  • Arrive Almaty — re-check into city hotel
  • Evening at leisure
Overnight in Almaty
🏛
Day 6
City Highlights & Final Shopping
  • Morning city tour — Zenkov Cathedral in Panfilov Park (1907, earthquake-proof wooden structure), Republic Square and the Central State Museum
  • Green Bazaar — dried fruits, nuts, spices, Kazakh dairy products
  • Rakhat Chocolate Factory — shopping stop for Kazakhstan's iconic confectionery brand
  • Late afternoon cable car to Kok-Tobe Hill — panoramic views over Almaty and the Tian Shan range at sunset
Overnight in Almaty
🛫
Day 7
Departure
  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Private transfer to Almaty International Airport
  • Departure

Day by Day
Itinerary
✈
Day 1
Arrival & Leisure
  • Arrive at Almaty International Airport — meet and greet on arrival
  • Private transfer to city-centre hotel and check-in
  • Full day at leisure — rest and recover from the flight
  • Optional: evening stroll on Arbat pedestrian street or Panfilov Park
Overnight in Almaty
🏔
Day 2
Medeu & Shymbulak Mountain Resort
  • Morning drive up Medeu Gorge to the iconic Medeu ice rink at 1,691m — the world's highest skating rink
  • 3-tiered scenic cable car ascent to Shymbulak Mountain Resort — views of the Zailiysky Alatau range throughout
  • Reach Level 3 at 3,200m — snow activities (seasonal), alpine views, mountain restaurant
  • Gondola ride back down — afternoon at leisure in the city
Overnight in Almaty
🏛
Day 3
City Tour & Kok-Tobe Sunset
  • Republic Square — the grand civic centre of Almaty, flanked by the Central State Museum
  • Panfilov Park — Zenkov Cathedral, one of the world's tallest wooden structures (built 1907, no nails)
  • Green Bazaar — the city's largest covered market, filled with dried fruits, spices, and local produce
  • Rakhat Chocolate Factory — Kazakhstan's iconic confectionery brand, shop and tasting stop
  • Afternoon cable car to Kok-Tobe Hill — alpine coaster, city panorama, and sunset views over the Tian Shan
Overnight in Almaty
🏞
Day 4
Lake Day Excursion
  • Full-day excursion to either Big Almaty Lake (2,510m altitude, 28km from city) or Lake Issyk — decided based on season and road conditions
  • Big Almaty Lake: turquoise glacial water against 4,000m+ peaks — accessible by road with a short walk to the shore
  • Lake Issyk: lower altitude, easier access, forested valley setting — a gentler option for mixed groups
  • Picnic lunch in the mountains; return to Almaty in the evening
Overnight in Almaty
🛫

Good news: The Azerbaijan ASAN e-Visa is one of the most straightforward visa processes available to Indian passport holders. There are no bank statements, no ITR documents, no interview, and no embassy visit required. Just three simple items and we handle the rest.
1
What We Need From You
Three items — that's all
🛂
Passport — Front & Back Bio Pages
Clear scans of both the photo/data page and the back page of your passport. The scan must be legible — no shadows, no cut edges, full page in frame. JPEG or PDF accepted. Passport must have a minimum of 6 months validity remaining from your travel date.
🏨
Accommodation Details
Hotel name and address in Baku (or wherever you're staying on the first night). If you're booked through Caravan Compass, we will provide this. If you've booked accommodation independently, share the booking confirmation.
💼
Occupation
Your current occupation or job title as it should appear on the visa application — for example: "Business Owner", "Software Engineer", "Homemaker", "Retired". No documents required; just confirm what to enter.
⚡
That's it
No bank statements. No ITR. No salary slips. No travel insurance requirement. No cover letter. Once we have these three items, we handle the entire application process.

3
How the Process Works
From document submission to visa in hand
1
Share your three documents with us
Send the passport scans, accommodation details, and your occupation via WhatsApp or email. We'll confirm receipt and let you know which processing tier you've selected.
2
We submit the application on the ASAN portal
We handle the full submission on the official evisa.gov.az portal. You don't need to create an account or navigate the portal yourself.
3
Visa is issued and shared with you
Once approved, the e-Visa is issued as a PDF. We share it with you directly — print it or save it digitally to present at the airport. Both printout and digital copy are accepted at Heydar Aliyev Airport.
4
Arrive in Baku — no further formalities
Present your e-Visa and passport at immigration. Processing is typically smooth and quick. Transfer to city centre takes 25–30 minutes from Heydar Aliyev International Airport.
📋
What to Carry at the Airport
Have your e-Visa printout or PDF and passport accessible before reaching the immigration counter. Baku immigration officers are efficient — having your documents ready avoids any delays. Return flight tickets and accommodation confirmation may occasionally be asked for; keep them accessible on your phone.
